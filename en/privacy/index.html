Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information we collect

Prep DELE C2 collects the minimum information necessary to provide the service:

  • Account data: If you create an account, we store your email address for authentication.
  • Progress data: Your practice exam progress is stored locally on your device and, if you have an account, synced securely.
  • Anonymous usage data: We collect anonymous analytics data (pages visited, features used) via Google Analytics to improve the app.

2. Information we do NOT collect

  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or other device data.

3. Subscriptions and payments

Payments are processed exclusively through Apple (App Store). We do not have access to your payment information (credit card, etc.). Apple manages all transactions according to their own privacy policy.

4. Data storage

Your progress data is stored primarily on your device. If you create an account, data is synced via secure cloud services with encryption in transit and at rest.

5. Data deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the app (Account → Delete Account). You can also request deletion by emailing hello@prepdele.com.

6. Third-party services

  • Google Analytics: For anonymous usage analytics.
  • Firebase: For authentication and data storage.
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management.

7. Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, email us at hello@prepdele.com.
