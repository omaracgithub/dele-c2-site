DELE C2 Sample Exam

The DELE C2 is the highest level of the Diploma of Spanish as a Foreign Language. Unlike other levels, the C2 integrates language skills into combined tests. Here's the complete structure.

Test 1: Use of Language, Reading and Listening Comprehension

Duration: 105 minutes · Tasks: 6

Task 1 — Reading comprehension

Answer multiple-choice questions about a complex informational text of approximately 450 words.

Task 2 — Reading comprehension

Complete a text with extracted fragments, demonstrating understanding of textual cohesion.

Task 3 — Reading comprehension

Identify which texts contain specific ideas or information.

Task 4 — Use of language

Complete a text with the correct lexical or grammatical options.

Task 5 — Listening comprehension

Answer questions about extended conversations or monologues.

Task 6 — Listening comprehension

Match statements with speakers or identify specific information in complex audio.

Test 2: Integrated Skills — Listening and Reading Comprehension + Written Expression

Duration: 150 minutes · Tasks: 3

Task 1 — Writing from listening

Listen to a lecture or presentation and write a formal text (report, article) integrating the information heard.

Task 2 — Writing from reading

Read several texts and write an argumentative text integrating ideas from the sources.

Task 3 — Revision and correction

Identify and correct errors in a text, demonstrating mastery of linguistic norms.

Test 3: Integrated Skills — Reading Comprehension + Oral Expression

Duration: 20 minutes (+ 30 min preparation) · Tasks: 3

Task 1 — Sustained monologue

Present a topic based on support texts, organizing a coherent and well-structured discourse.

Task 2 — Debate

Discuss the presented topic with the interviewer, defending and nuancing opinions.

Task 3 — Negotiation

Participate in a communicative situation requiring negotiation, persuasion, or problem-solving.

Scoring

Grading system

Maximum score: 100 points. A minimum of 60% is required on each test (20 points per test) to pass. The result is PASS or FAIL.
